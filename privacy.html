Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Scope

MakeMD Postiz ("the Service") is a private, self-hosted scheduling tool used exclusively by its operator, the music artist MakeMD. It has no third-party users and therefore collects no personal data from the public.

2. What data the Service handles

  • OAuth tokens for the Operator's own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Telegram, Threads). Tokens are stored on the Operator's own server and are used only to publish and manage the Operator's content.
  • Basic profile data of the Operator's own accounts (username, avatar, follower statistics) as returned by the platforms' official APIs, used only to display account status inside the Service.
  • Media files created by the Operator, stored on the Operator's own infrastructure for publishing.

3. What the Service does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, or process data of any person other than the Operator.
  • It does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • It does not use advertising, analytics trackers, or cookies for visitors of this site.

4. Data retention and deletion

All data lives on the Operator's own server and can be deleted by the Operator at any time by disconnecting an account or deleting the self-hosted instance. Revoking the app's access in TikTok settings (Settings → Security → Apps and services) immediately invalidates its access.

5. Contact

Privacy questions: contact the Operator via the links on the home page.
