MakeMD Postiz

MakeMD Postiz is a self-hosted social media scheduling tool operated by the music artist MakeMD. It publishes the artist's own music videos and teasers to the artist's own social accounts (including TikTok) on a schedule.

The tool is private, single-user, and non-commercial: it does not serve third-party users, does not collect data from anyone other than the operating artist, and posts only content owned by the artist.
